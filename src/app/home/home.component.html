Warcraft Legends

Warcraft Legends is a dedicated community site that hosts high quality reviews to showcase the excellent work of the warcraft community. Warcraft 3 is an iconic Real Time Strategy game that has been around since 2003. Blizzard released the assets for the game on launch and gave the community a game engine environment to work with. Thanks to these resources and the creativity of content creators from the community, we have enjoyed unique custom community made games for 18 years. The ideas of these creators live on and have contributed to booming million-dollar gaming genres like the MOBA genre.
